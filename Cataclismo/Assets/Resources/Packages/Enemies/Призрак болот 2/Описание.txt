Backstory:
A spectral knight fused with swamp silt by the elemental surge, the Siltwraith Crusader guards a lost altar. It deems intruders profane, wielding earthen magic to bury them in mud to cleanse the mire’s corruption.

Dialogue Upon Encountering Player:
“Heretic! Your presence defiles the mire! Sink into silt!”

Attacks:





Silt Vortex: Summons a muddy whirlwind, high damage, slows movement.



Clay Cage: Traps player in hardening clay, immobilizing, moderate damage.



Mire Tremor: Slams ground, high damage, disorients.
