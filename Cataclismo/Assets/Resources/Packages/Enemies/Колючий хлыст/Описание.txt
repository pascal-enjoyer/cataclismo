Backstory:
Worm-like creatures of hardened mud, Sludgescale Wurms burrow in swamp muck, nibbling magical residue. They’re slow and fragile, posing little danger.

Dialogue Upon Encountering Player:
“Squirm! Magic… mine…!”

Attacks:





Mud Spit: Spits muck, low damage.



Wriggle Ram: Rams player, minimal damage.
