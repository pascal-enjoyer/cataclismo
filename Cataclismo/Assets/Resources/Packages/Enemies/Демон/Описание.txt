Delicate, flower-like humanoids sprouted from swamp muck, Mirebloom Sprouts seek magical energy to grow. They’re fragile, wilting under attack, posing minimal threat.

Petal Toss: Throws toxic petals, low damage.



Vine Flick: Whips vine, minimal damage.
