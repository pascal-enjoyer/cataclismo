Backstory:
Tiny plant-like humanoids sprouted from swamp muck, Muckling Sprigs wander in clusters, seeking magical traces. They’re frail, scattering when struck, posing little threat.

Dialogue Upon Encountering Player:
“Rustle! Magic… yummy…!”

Attacks:





Sludge Flick: Tosses muck, low damage, slows slightly.



Nibble: Bites, minimal damage.
